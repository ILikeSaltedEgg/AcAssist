How to Get a True Copy of Grades

Follow these steps to secure your official true copy of grades efficiently.

Step 1: Prepare the Required Documents

Ensure you have your student ID, registration form, and any required application forms.

Step 2: Visit the Registrar's Office

Head to your university's registrar's office during office hours to submit your documents.

Step 3: Pay the Processing Fee

Follow the payment process as instructed by your university. Keep the receipt for reference.

Step 4: Collect Your True Copy of Grades

Wait for processing time and collect your official copy as advised.
